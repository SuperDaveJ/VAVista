Kernel Toolkit's ^XINDEX utility  -- formerly known as %INDEX -- utility is a verification tool developers can use to review routines and embedded M code.

It reviews routines and embedded M code in data dictionaries, functions, and templates according to the 1995 ANSI M Standard and according to the VAProgramming Standards and Conventions, or SAC.

^XINDEX reports any errors that it finds. The reported error codes, or error flags,  are as follows:

  • S — Standards violation -- according to VA Standards
  • W — Warning violation --according to VA Conventions, and
  •  F — Fatal M error, also called a hard M error
