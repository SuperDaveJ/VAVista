VistA applications utilize data files maintained in the VA FileManager.

Within these files are the clinical, administrative, and computer infrastructure data supporting day-to-day operations and containing patients’ medical and healthcare usage histories, including data on:

  • Demographics
  • Episodes of care
  • Medicines
  • Practitioner information
  • Diagnoses
  • Procedures

All patients treated at VA Medical Centers are included in the files, which are updated continuously at the point of care or as part of administrative processes.
