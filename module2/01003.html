  • VistA applications utilize data files maintained using the VA FileManager.
  • Each data file contains clinical, administrative, and computer infrastructure data that support day-to-day operations and comprises patients’ medical and healthcare usage histories, including data on:
    • Demographics
    • Episodes of care
    • Medicines
    • Practitioner information
    • Diagnoses
    • Procedures
  • All patients treated at VA Medical Centers are included in the files.
  • Files are updated continuously at the point of care or as part of administrative processes.
